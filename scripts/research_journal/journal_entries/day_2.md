**Day 2 Entry, Outline**
- Acacia sleeps really well and gets up shortly after sunrise.
- She only woke up once durng the night, and saw a gastly floating around the island. Most people would be a bit freaked out but acacia has worked with ghost pokemon before and didnt really suspect any bad intensions.
- Still no hunger or thirst. Seems safe to say this strange realm likely will prevent them from being a problem indefinately at this point. Whether it is altering the way reality works entirely to accomplish this or is simply directly sustaining her is unclear at this juncture.
- She really had nothing else to do besides start researching this island and learn what she can about this entire new reality. She didn't have one of those fancy Rotom smartphones with games on it or anything, having decided to use only a traditional Pokédex and flip phone instead a while back in order to not be online all the time.
- The research might have been more tantalizing anyways. She decides to start with the bin, the only part of this small island she didnt properly explore yesterday.
